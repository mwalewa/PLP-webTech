Alissan Investment Limited provides premier quality services, products and consultancy in various areas, like construction, marketing and communication, and ICT services. The company was established and incorporated in the year 2022 starting as the consultancy wing of its then parent company Beaconhill Investment Limited before becoming a fully fledged independent company with its headquater in Nairobi.
